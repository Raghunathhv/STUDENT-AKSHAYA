  • Summarize topics in your own words.
  • Use mind maps, charts, and bullet points.
  • Highlight key terms and formulas.
  • Focus on understanding, not memorizing.
  • Watch videos or ask teachers for clarity.
  • Use real-life examples to relate topics.
  • Revise weekly to strengthen memory.
  • Use flashcards and formula sheets.
  • Take quick quizzes or self-tests.
  • Solve model papers and past question papers.
  • Understand the exam pattern and time management.
  • Identify frequently asked questions.
  • Read question paper carefully before starting.
  • Attempt easy questions first, then tough ones.
  • Keep last 10–15 mins for revision.
